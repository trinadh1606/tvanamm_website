Tea Franchise FAQs

What is the minimum investment required for a tea franchise in India?
The minimum investment starts from ₹2 Lakhs for a kiosk tea stall franchise. Café formats require ₹8–15 Lakhs, while master franchises start from ₹20 Lakhs. We share a transparent breakup covering setup, equipment, branding and initial stock.
How much space is needed for a tea shop or chai café franchise?
Kiosk formats need 50–100 sq ft while chai cafés require 200–500 sq ft. We help with site evaluation, layout guidance and store design for optimal operations.
Do you provide marketing and ongoing support after launch?
Yes. We provide centralized procurement, branding materials, local marketing toolkits, quality audits, and new product rollouts along with periodic business reviews.
Which regions do you currently serve?
We actively serve Hyderabad, Telangana and Andhra Pradesh, and are expanding to South & West India. Enquire for availability in your city.
What is the expected ROI timeline?
Most partners recover investment within 8–18 months depending on location, rent, format and operational discipline. Kiosk formats typically achieve faster payback.
